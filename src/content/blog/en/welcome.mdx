After 15 years building software, I decided to open this space to share what I
learn along the way. Here I will write about **PHP**, application architecture,
best practices and anything I find useful.

## Why a blog without a database?

This blog is built with [Astro](https://astro.build) and Markdown/MDX. Each
article is a file, which means:

- **Zero database** to maintain.
- **Maximum speed**: everything is generated as static HTML.
- **Version control** with Git for every post.
